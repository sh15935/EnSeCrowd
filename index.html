WHO WE ARE

Welcome to the EnSeCrowd Intelligence Club at ENSA Tangier. We are a team of passionate engineering students dedicated to cybersecurity and cyber intelligence, working together to make the digital world safer.

Our mission is to train the next generation of cybersecurity experts — ethical, skilled, and ready to protect information systems from modern threats.

Supported by professionals and mentors, we combine hands-on practice with strong theoretical foundations, empowering our members to thrive in the cybersecurity ecosystem.

Our Mission

"Educate. Empower. Secure."

At EnSeCrowd, we strive to:

  • Strengthen cybersecurity awareness and digital hygiene across our community.
  • Provide high-quality training, workshops, and conferences guided by professionals.
  • Build a culture of ethics, innovation, and resilience in every aspect of cybersecurity.

Our goal is to create an environment where digital security is not just a skill — it's a mindset.

Our Vision

The EnSeCrowd Club at ENSA Tangier aims to become a key cybersecurity hub in Northern Morocco.

We achieve this by offering innovative training programs, competitive hacking challenges, and real-world simulations that prepare our members for the complex challenges of the cyber world.

We value Collaboration, Ethics, and Responsibility, and believe these principles are essential to shaping trustworthy cybersecurity professionals.
